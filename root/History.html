Our History


Evolved from a basement in Stirling in 1983, McLinSoft is a small technical support company consisting of 20 persons spread over 3 locations in Scotland: Glasgow [HQ], Edinburgh and Aberdeen.

Whist three quarters of their staff are remote/site workers, the remainder are situated across the 3 offices, responsible for general business administration and client liaison.

McLinSoft have a 5-year business plan involving the expansion of their business and subsequent increase in staff, product & services manifesto and client portfolio.

As of 2017, McLinsoft has been engaged by ClydeSpace in a joint venture to open the first livable workspace on the Moon.
